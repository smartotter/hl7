Fusion Test 3540

In this test, the Evidence Creator creates a Blended Softcopy
Presentation State (BSPS) object that specifies how to blend for
display two series of images.  These series were created on a hybrid
Acquisition Modality and have the same Frame of Reference.
This tests transaction [RAD-57] in the Image Fusion Profile and
Storage Commitment [RAD-10].  Spatial Registration is tested in another test.
